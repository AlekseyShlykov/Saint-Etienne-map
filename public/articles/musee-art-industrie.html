Museum of Art and Industry

The Musée d’Art et d’Industrie (MAI) in Saint-Étienne is one of the main museums of the city. It is housed in a striking 19th-century building and tells the story of the three industries that made Saint-Étienne famous: ribbons, arms, and cycles.

The ribbon collections include looms, samples, and fashion pieces that illustrate the city’s role as a global centre for ribbon production. The arms section covers military and civilian weapons, while the cycle department traces the evolution of the bicycle and its local industry.

Temporary exhibitions and educational workshops make the MAI a living museum. The building itself, with its grand staircase and period décor, is worth a visit.
