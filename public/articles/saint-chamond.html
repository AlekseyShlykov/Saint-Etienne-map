Saint-Chamond Town Hall

Saint-Chamond is a town in the Loire department, north-east of Saint-Étienne. Its town hall and the surrounding historic centre are representative of the area’s industrial past and its current role as a lively suburban town.

The belfry and the pedestrian streets around the town hall host markets and local events. The town has preserved several industrial and religious heritage sites.

A short drive or tram ride from Saint-Étienne, Saint-Chamond is a good starting point for exploring the Gier valley and the Pilat regional natural park.
